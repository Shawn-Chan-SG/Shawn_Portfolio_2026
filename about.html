Professional Profile

A commercially driven management executive with over 20 years of experience building and scaling operations across Singapore, Vietnam and the wider Asia-Pacific region.

Currently Country Manager at ROCKWOOL Building Materials (Singapore) Pte Ltd, I oversee full P&L responsibility for the Singapore market. Prior to this, I served as Director of ROCKWOOL Khai Hoan JSC in Vietnam — a role where I led a 130-person organisation and delivered a doubling of revenue within 12 months.

My career spans business development, product management, technical services, commercial excellence and marketing operations. I thrive at the intersection of strategy and execution: crafting go-to-market plans, driving M&A integration (including the acquisition of Bestofire and Thermal Pte Ltd in 2020), and building high-performing, cross-functional teams.

A lifelong learner, I hold degrees from the University of South Australia, NUS Business School and Singapore Management University (MBA, 2026). I believe that rigorous analysis, clear communication and genuine curiosity about people are the foundation of effective leadership.
